We provide opportunities for recreational and competitive soccer development that enhance character, community and love of the game.

To ignite and grow a lifelong passion for the game of soccer.

  • TEAM: The Club’s TEAM is comprised of players, members, staff, coaches, referees, volunteers.
  • ACCOUNTABILITY: We hold our TEAM accountable for their actions on and off the field in upholding the vision and mission of the Club.
  • COMPETITIVENESS: Delivering results with the resources available is of the utmost importance.
  • EVOLUTION: We are open to responsible change for the betterment of the Club.
  • RESPECT: We demand ethical behavior of ourselves on and off the field.
  • RESPONSIBILITY: We advocate vigorously and responsibly for our TEAM.
  • SAFETY: The safety of our youth is the TEAM’s number one priority.
  • SPORTSMANSHIP: We foster a positive, collaborative and respectful environment.
  • VALUE: We treat each other as valued colleagues.
  • VOICE: Everyone has a voice which will be heard and treated with respect, professionalism and timeliness.
